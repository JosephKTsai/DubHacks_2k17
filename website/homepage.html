Experience life from someone else's world

Our Mission: Illuminate the WORLD

We believe that the best way to experience someone else's life is to experience it in their shoes.

Life is comprised of both the exciting and the everyday, and we believe that experiencing both from someone else's life is a great way to begin an understanding of their culture.

We want to illuminate the world through offering our users the chance to experience other cultures through the lives of every day people. Just like us, and just like you.

So, How do we Do it?

1. You enter in your information

2. You choose the culture you want to experience

3. You share culture for a week with someone from around the world
